The Company

Inductive is an engineering consulting firm established to provide transformative engineering solutions for clients in heavy industry dealing with electrification projects. We capture opportunity and value through the appropriate development and application of hardware and predictive modeling.

Based in Seattle, Washington, Inductive has built a network of peers to cover areas of expertise beyond electrical engineering and product strategy — including materials, mechanical engineering, software security, and power electronics.

Core Focus Areas

  • Power electronics and electrical infrastructure
  • Electrification of heavy-industry mobile equipment
  • Remote data collection and analysis pipelines for asset health monitoring, predictive maintenance, and utilization maximization
